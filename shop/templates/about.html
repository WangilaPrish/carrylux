About Carrylux

Carrylux is Kenya's premier online destination for authentic designer bags and luxury brands. We curate the finest selection of handbags, totes, backpacks, and accessories from top global designers, ensuring quality, style, and exclusivity for every customer.

Our Story

Founded in Nairobi, Carrylux was born from a passion for fashion and a desire to bring world-class luxury to East Africa. Today, we serve thousands of happy customers and continue to grow our collection and community.
